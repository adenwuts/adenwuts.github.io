Overview of Event Related Potential (ERP)

A commonly used time domain anaylsis method that functions by averaging out single signals from multiple trials. The average tends to discard a lot of the variability present in a single trial which decreases noise at the cost of information.

It is sometimes argued that ERP only quantify a very small portion of the information that exists within EEG data.

Information Loss in Time-Domain Averaging
